## Question

Design the encounter-resolution system: how the auto-battler core works (what triggers, in what order, over what timeline) and what the one captain decision per round is (what options it presents, what it affects, how/when the player makes it). This system must be resolvable deterministically, since it needs to run identically in PvE and ghost-based PvP, and in headless simulation.
